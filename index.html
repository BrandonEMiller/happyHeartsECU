Are you depressed or feeling down most of the time?

You may be eligible to receive FREE therapy for your depression.

If any of the following apply to you, then consider taking part in our study:

  • Diabetic
  • Overweight
  • A smoker
  • Have high blood pressure
  • Concerned about your risk for cardiovascular/heart disease

We are Seeking Adults with depression who are not currently taking antidepressant medication to be part of a research study at ECU.

More Information: Eligible participants will receive 12 weeks of an effective depression treatment involving weekly meetings with a therapist. This treatment will guide participants to make changes in their daily lives that will improve the way they feel.
