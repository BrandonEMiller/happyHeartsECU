Happy Heart Study

Are you depressed or feeling down most of the time?

You may be eligible to receive FREE therapy for your depression.

Is this study right for me?

If you are not taking any medication for depression

AND

if you have one or more of the health concerns listed below, then you may be a great fit

  • Diabetic
  • Overweight
  • Chronic smoking
  • High blood pressure
  • Concerns about your risk for cardiovascular/heart disease

More Information

You would do a short phone interview followed by an in-person screening to determine if you are eligible to be a participant.

Participants will receive depression treatment for 12 weeks by attending therapy sessions with a psychologist. This treatment will guide participants to make changes in their daily life that will improve the way they feel.

This is not an experimental treatment. Prior research has shown it to be effective for most people. Our study seeks to determine if depression treatment reduces risk for cardiovascular/heart disease.
